Privacy Policy for Meal Mates

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Meal Mates ("we," "our," or "the app") is a calorie- and nutrition-tracking app with social accountability features. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. By using Meal Mates, you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

When you create an account and use Meal Mates, we collect:

  • Account information: your email address, username, and name.
  • Profile details you provide: date of birth, sex, height, weight, activity level, and your goal (lose, maintain, or gain weight). These are used to calculate your recommended calorie and macronutrient targets.
  • Food and activity data: the foods you log (including calories and macronutrients), workouts you record, and meal plans you create.
  • Profile photo: if you choose to upload one.
  • Friend connections: the friend requests you send and accept, and the connections between your account and other users' accounts.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the app's core features: tracking calories, macros, workouts, and goals.
  • Calculate your personalized calorie and nutrition targets.
  • Let you connect with friends and, with your participation, show your daily totals to friends you are connected with for accountability.
  • Maintain and improve the app.

Sharing With Friends

Meal Mates includes social accountability features. When you connect with another user as friends, your daily totals (such as calories, macros, and logged workouts) may be visible to that friend within the app. You choose who to connect with by sending and accepting friend requests.

How Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, our backend and database provider. Access to your data is protected by authentication and database security rules. We do not sell your personal information.

Third-Party Services

Meal Mates uses the following third-party services to function:

  • Supabase — account authentication and data storage.
  • USDA FoodData Central — to look up nutrition information for foods you search.
  • Open Food Facts — to look up nutrition information when you scan a product barcode.

When you search for foods or scan barcodes, the relevant query is sent to these services to return nutrition data.

Your Choices

  • You can edit your profile information at any time within the app.
  • You can remove friend connections at any time.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

Meal Mates is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
